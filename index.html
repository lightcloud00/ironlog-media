FAQ

Got Questions?

What exercises are included?

56 exercises across 7 muscle groups: Chest (8), Back (8), Shoulders (8), Arms (8), Legs (8), Core (8), and Cardio (8). Every major compound and the most effective isolation exercises.

How does AI progressive overload work?

The AI analyzes your workout history — sets, reps, weight, rest times — and calculates the optimal load for your next session. It follows proven periodization principles to ensure consistent gains without overtraining.

Is my data secure?

Workout data stays on your device by default. Partner matching data is encrypted end-to-end. We don't sell your data — ever. Privacy by design.

How does gym partner matching work?

Check in when you arrive at the gym. Browse other lifters at your gym or nearby. Filter by workout style (powerlifting, hypertrophy, cardio), experience level, and schedule. Send a training invite — no social feed, no drama.

What platforms do you support?

iOS (App Store) and Apple Watch are live now. Android is in development and coming soon to Google Play.

Can I really pay once and own it forever?

Yes. The $29.99 lifetime option gives you Pro features forever. No recurring charges. No bait-and-switch. If you prefer, monthly ($4.99) and annual ($39.99) options are available too.
