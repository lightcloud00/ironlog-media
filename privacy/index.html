IRON LOG

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 6, 2026

1. What IronLog stores

The current public IronLog release is a local-first workout tracker. Your workout history, exercises, notes, supported training entries, and stats are stored on your device so the app can function without an account.

2. Apple Health (HealthKit) data

If you choose to enable Apple Health integration, IronLog reads and writes workout-related data only:

  • Read scope: workout sessions, active energy burned, and body weight entries you have already logged in Apple Health, used solely to display alongside your IronLog workouts.
  • Write scope: workouts you complete in IronLog can be saved back to Apple Health as workout records, only when you explicitly opt in.
  • Retention: HealthKit data IronLog reads is held in app memory only for the current session and is not copied to any server, backup, or analytics pipeline.
  • Sharing: HealthKit data is never shared with third parties, advertisers, or analytics providers. It is never used to derive marketing audiences.
  • Revoking access: you can disable IronLog's Apple Health permissions at any time in iOS Settings → Health → Data Access & Devices → IronLog.

Apple Health integration is optional. IronLog functions fully as a local workout tracker without it.

3. Account requirements

The current public release does not require account creation for core workout tracking.

4. Support messages

If you email support, we receive the contact information and message you choose to send so we can respond.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell your workout data. We do not run third-party analytics, ad SDKs, or tracking pixels in the app. For the current public release, your workout history and supported training entries stay on-device unless you choose to export or share them from a supported build.

6. Your choices

  • You can delete app data by removing the app from your device.
  • You can export workout history when export tools are available in your installed build.
  • You can revoke Apple Health permissions in iOS Settings at any time.
  • You can contact gus@gusdigitalsolutions.com with privacy questions.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to gus@gusdigitalsolutions.com.
