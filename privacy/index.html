IRON LOG

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 11, 2026

1. What IronLog stores

The current public IronLog release is a local-first workout tracker. Your workout history, exercises, notes, supported training entries, and stats are stored on your device so the app can function without an account.

2. Account requirements

The current public release does not require account creation for core workout tracking.

3. Support and website contact messages

If you email support or submit the website contact form, we receive the contact information, technical metadata such as user agent, and message you choose to send so we can respond. Website contact form submissions use our first-party website function and site storage when available, and the form can fall back to an email draft if direct form delivery is unavailable. Contact messages are intended for support follow-up and routine cleanup.

4. Subscriptions and in-app purchases

IronLog offers IronLog Pro, an optional auto-renewable subscription with monthly and annual plans. Subscription purchases are processed by Apple's in-app purchase system, and we do not receive or store your full payment card or billing details.

StoreKit verifies purchases on-device so the app can unlock IronLog Pro features and restore access on eligible devices signed into the same Apple ID. We do not use subscription receipts for advertising or third-party analytics.

Apple controls billing, renewal, cancellation, and refund handling under Apple's subscription policies. You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime at https://apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell your workout data. We do not run third-party analytics, ad SDKs, or tracking pixels in the app. For the current public release, your workout history and supported training entries stay on-device unless you choose to send information to support.

6. Your choices

  • You can delete app data by removing the app from your device.
  • You can contact gus@gusdigitalsolutions.com with privacy questions or to request deletion of website contact messages you sent.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to gus@gusdigitalsolutions.com.
