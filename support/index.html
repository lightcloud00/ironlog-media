Current public release

The current public build is an iPhone App Store workout tracker focused on logging sets, viewing history, reviewing stats, and keeping training data on-device. iOS follow-up work is active. No Android or Google Play release is available today.

Quick answers

  • No account is required for the current public release.
  • Workout history and supported training entries are stored on your device.
  • If the app feels out of date, install the latest version from the App Store and relaunch it.

Roadmap questions

If you're asking about PR-tracker work, extra history detail, or other upcoming features, mention that in your email and include your installed app version if available.
