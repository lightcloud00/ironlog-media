1. Using IronLog

IronLog is provided as a workout tracking tool. Use it responsibly and at your own discretion.

2. Subscriptions and pricing

IronLog is free to download and use for core personal record tracking. IronLog Pro is an optional auto-renewable subscription offered through Apple's in-app purchase system, with monthly and annual plans. Pricing is shown in the app before purchase, may vary by storefront, and may change with notice.

3. Auto-renewal and cancellation

Subscriptions renew automatically unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period. Your Apple ID will be charged within 24 hours prior to renewal at the price shown in the app. You can manage your subscription, view renewal status, or cancel anytime in your Apple ID account settings at https://apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions.

Canceling stops future renewals but does not refund the current billing period. Access continues until the current period ends.

4. Refunds

Refunds are handled by Apple under Apple's refund policies. To request a refund, visit https://reportaproblem.apple.com or contact Apple Support. IronLog does not process refunds directly because we do not receive or store your payment information.

5. What IronLog Pro unlocks

An active IronLog Pro subscription unlocks CSV export of training history, the built-in plate calculator, the in-workout rest timer, and custom movement categories. Core workout logging, the exercise library, history views, and local-first storage remain available in the free app.

IronLog is not medical advice, physical therapy, or one-on-one coaching. Always use proper judgment and seek professional guidance when appropriate.
